Balance - Mindfulness - Togetherness

Our vision is about empowering transformation through education – we want to use yoga and mindful movement as a force for good. It’s our mission to support you as you connect with your unique state of being through education and the practise of yoga and mindfulness. We want every body to feel they are a part of something that we know is making a collective difference.

Balance

LuJong

Lu (Tibetan: body) Jong (exercises) are meditative practice sequences. They promote mindfulness and concentration. In their basic structure, they are easy to learn and suitable for all age groups.

TsaLung

Tsa (Tibetan: body channels) Lung (flow of energy, breath) is a powerful, deep practice with great healing potential.

Mindfulness

Meditation

Meditation is a central tool in BodyMind training. Meditation enables us to discover and cultivate our own silence, boundless space and radiance, beyond thoughts and emotions.

Mind Balancing

Mind balancing teaches how the mind works. We learn to control it, train it and promote its perceptual abilities.

Togetherness

Monthly group social sessions that are free of charge in the summer as we will meet in either a public park or forest, with a small fee in winter as an indoor area will be needed. Group social sessions take place most Saturdays at 10:00 am for a total of 45 min. Activity, locations and dates get updated monthly.
